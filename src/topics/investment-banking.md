# Investment Banking

Investment banking is a specialized sector of the financial industry that focuses on providing advisory and capital-raising services to corporations, governments, and other institutions. Investment banks assist clients in mergers and acquisitions (M&A), underwriting new securities offerings, facilitating trading and market-making activities, and offering strategic financial advice.
