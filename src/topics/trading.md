# Trading

Trading refers to the buying and selling of financial instruments, such as stocks, bonds, commodities, currencies, and derivatives, with the aim of generating profits. Trading can occur in various markets, including stock exchanges, commodity markets, and foreign exchange markets. Traders use different strategies and techniques to capitalize on price movements and market trends.
