# Banking Regulation

## Regulatory Bodies

| Country | Regulatory Body Acronym | Full Name of Regulatory Body | Primary Responsibilities |
|----------|-------------------------|------------------------------|---------------------------|
| **UK** | FCA | Financial Conduct Authority | Oversees financial firms, ensuring consumer protection and market integrity. |
|  | PRA | Prudential Regulation Authority | Focuses on the prudential aspects of banking, ensuring stability and soundness. |
| **Switzerland** | FINMA | Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority | Supervises banks, insurance companies, stock exchanges, and securities dealers. |
| **Austria** | FMA | Financial Market Authority | Supervises banks, insurance companies, pensions, and securities markets. |
| **Germany** | BaFin | Federal Financial Supervisory Authority | Supervises banks, financial services institutions, and insurance enterprises. |
| **Hong Kong** | HKMA | Hong Kong Monetary Authority | Acts as the central banking institution, managing monetary policy and banking regulation. |
| **Singapore** | MAS | Monetary Authority of Singapore | Functions as both the central bank and the financial regulatory authority. |

## Regulations

| Country | Regulation Name | Description | Regulatory Body |
|----------|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **UK** | Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (FSMA) | Framework for managing and supervising the financial services industry. | FCA |
|  | Banking Act 2009 | Enhances the resilience and resolvability of banks. | PRA |
|  | Consumer Credit Act 1974 | Protects consumers and governs the rights and duties of creditors. | FCA |
|  | Payment Services Regulations 2017 | Regulates payment services and payment service providers. | FCA |
|  | Mortgages and Home Finance: Conduct of Business (MCOB) | Detailed rules for firms dealing with mortgage and home finance products. | FCA |
|  | Prudential Sourcebook (BIPRU) | Prudential regulations for banks, building societies, and investment firms. | PRA |
|  | Anti-Money Laundering Regulations 2017 | Details procedures to prevent money laundering. | FCA |
| **Switzerland** | Federal Act on Banks and Savings Banks | Governs the licensing and supervision of banks. | FINMA |
|  | Anti-Money Laundering Act | Prevents money laundering and financing of terrorism. | FINMA |
|  | Data Protection Act | Regulates the processing of personal data by private parties and federal bodies. | FDPIC |
|  | Collective Investment Schemes Act | Regulates collective investment schemes and their managers. | FINMA |
| **Austria** | Bankwesengesetz (BWG) - Banking Act | Governs banking activities, including licensing and supervision. | FMA |
|  | Wertpapieraufsichtsgesetz (WAG) - Securities Supervision Act | Regulates securities and markets. | FMA |
|  | Zahlungsdienstegesetz (ZaDiG) - Payment Services Act | Supervises payment services. | FMA |
| **Germany** | Kreditwesengesetz (KWG) - Banking Act | Main law governing the supervision of banks in Germany. | BaFin |
|  | Wertpapierhandelsgesetz (WpHG) - Securities Trading Act | Regulates the operation of securities markets. | BaFin |
|  | Zahlungsdiensteaufsichtsgesetz (ZAG) - Payment Services Oversight Act | Oversees payment services. | BaFin |
| **Hong Kong** | Banking Ordinance | Primary legislation that governs banks and banking practices. | HKMA |
|  | Securities and Futures Ordinance | Regulates securities and futures markets. | SFC |
|  | Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorist Financing Ordinance | Framework for AML and CTF. | HKMA |
| **Singapore** | Banking Act | Main legislation governing banking in Singapore. | MAS |
|  | Securities and Futures Act | Regulates activities and institutions in securities and futures markets. | MAS |
|  | Financial Advisers Act | Regulates the activities of financial advisers. | MAS |
|  | Payment Services Act 2019 | Regulates payment systems and payment service providers. | MAS |
