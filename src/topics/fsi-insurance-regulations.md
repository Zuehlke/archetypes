# Insurance Regulation

## Regulatory Bodies

| Country | Regulatory Body Acronym | Full Name | Primary Responsibilities |
|----------|-------------------------|------------|---------------------------|
| **UK** | FCA | Financial Conduct Authority | Regulates conduct of insurance businesses to ensure fair treatment of customers. |
|  | PRA | Prudential Regulation Authority | Responsible for prudential regulation of insurers, ensuring financial stability. |
| **Switzerland** | FINMA | Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority | Supervises insurance companies and enforces compliance with regulations. |
| **Austria** | FMA | Financial Market Authority | Regulates insurance companies and the insurance market. |
| **Germany** | BaFin | Federal Financial Supervisory Authority | Oversees insurance industry stability and legal compliance. |
| **Hong Kong** | IA | Insurance Authority | Regulates and supervises the insurance industry to promote stability. |
| **Singapore** | MAS | Monetary Authority of Singapore | Oversees the insurance sector as part of its broader mandate. |

---

## Regulations

| Country | Regulation Name | Description | Regulatory Body |
|----------|-----------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **UK** | Solvency II Directive | EU directive ensuring insurers have sufficient capital to cover risks. | PRA |
|  | Insurance Conduct of Business Sourcebook (ICOBS) | Rules on the conduct of insurance business. | FCA |
|  | The Insurance Companies Regulations 1994 | Specifies minimum solvency margins. | PRA |
|  | Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (FSMA) | Framework for all financial services regulation. | FCA |
| **Switzerland** | Swiss Insurance Supervision Act (ISA) | Governs licensing and operation of insurance companies. | FINMA |
|  | Swiss Insurance Contract Act | Regulates content and obligations in insurance contracts. | Federal Office of Private Insurance |
| **Austria** | Insurance Supervision Act | Ensures solvency and proper conduct in insurance markets. | FMA |
|  | Versicherungsvertragsgesetz (Insurance Contract Act) | Governs contracts between insurers and policyholders. | FMA |
|  | Austrian Insurance Tax Act | Regulates taxes related to insurance premiums. | Federal Ministry of Finance |
| **Germany** | Insurance Supervision Act (VAG) | Comprehensive insurance industry law. | BaFin |
|  | Solvency II | Ensures insurers maintain sufficient capital under stress scenarios. | BaFin |
|  | Insurance Contract Act (VVG) | Regulates rights and obligations within insurance contracts. | BaFin |
| **Hong Kong** | Insurance Ordinance | Main insurance legislation. | IA |
|  | Mandatory Provident Fund Schemes Ordinance | Regulates retirement insurance provisions. | MPFSA |
|  | Motor Vehicles Insurance (Third Party Risks) Ordinance | Regulates mandatory motor vehicle insurance. | IA |
| **Singapore** | Insurance Act | Governs insurance business in Singapore. | MAS |
|  | Risk-Based Capital Framework | Ensures insurers hold capital aligned with risk profiles. | MAS |
|  | Direct Life Insurer (Composite) Scheme | Allows combined life and general business under specific conditions. | MAS |
